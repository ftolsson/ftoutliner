Track characters

Select characters in the list to highlight scenes where they appear.
Selecting more than one will highlight scenes where ALL are present.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
  • Highlight your nine most frequent characters with keys 1—9.
  • Hold CMD to select two or more characters simultaneously
  • Deselect all highlights with 0 (zero).
  • Open (and dismiss) this dialog by pressing T for ”Tracking”.
TIP: You can avoid false positives by adding an empty note inside a name to hide it from being tracked:
”She grabs the gun Fra[[]]nk gave her”
