Known Times-of-Day

    In order to strip out the times-of-day from your scene headings, FTOutliner needs to know what they are. If you are writing in a language that is not represented below, add your own times-of-day to the list.

    Double-click on white space in any column to add a new item, or drag and drop to reorder existing ones.
    Group and sorting preferences 
  • When off, no or unknown times-of-day are colored red (and sorted in their own sheduling group).


  • When off, parentheticals like (MOVING) or (FLASHBACK) are not shown.
    • When both boxes are ON, grouping looks at parenthesis first, e.g. putting all (FLASHBACK) scenes in one group.
